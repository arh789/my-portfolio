## Core Artistic Themes to Pursue (Based on Dataset)

- **Clown as Seer & Truth-Teller**  
  - The fool who speaks hidden truths  
  - Grotesque laughter masking wisdom  
  - Duality of joy and menace

- **Decay & Grand Ruin**  
  - Faded opulence, forgotten grandeur  
  - Ornate, crumbling beauty—baroque ruin  
  - Time’s erosion of sacred and profane

- **Masks & Shifting Identity**  
  - The hidden self vs. the performed self  
  - Masks as protection, deception, and revelation  
  - Fragmented, shifting personas in motion

- **Sacred Symbols & Glyphs**  
  - Occult scripts and forbidden knowledge  
  - Ritualistic markings, distorted prophecy  
  - Sigil-like language within surreal environments

- **Flickering & Digital Decay**  
  - Reality breaking apart—glitches, distortion  
  - Faces flickering between states of being  
  - Decomposition revealing deeper layers

- **Cosmic Horror & Psychological Collapse**  
  - The infinite unknown, existential dread  
  - Madness encoded in symbols and faces  
  - Unstable perspectives—characters dissolving

- **Puppetry & Animated Ritual**  
  - Marionettes, broken automata, living dolls  
  - Stop-motion unease, jerky unnatural movement  
  - Ritualistic animation loops—endless cycles

- **Theatre of the Uncanny**  
  - Ballet, masquerade, gothic opera  
  - Performance as revelation and deception  
  - Elegance twisted into horror

Each theme reinforces transformation, deception, and the slow collapse of meaning, making them ideal for animation, masks, 3D sculpture, and experimental video production.
